VR-Showroom

Showcasing Possibl

One of the more interesting study-related projects I did was a collaboration between our program and a company from Cologne called World of VR. During the entire semester, our activities ranged from Marketing-related tasks, such as developing a strategy for going international, up to the final prototype my team developed, to showcase the company's product Possibl. I took the pictures on display here from that prototype.

The tasks related to this project were diverse, since the project lasted for an entire semester and spanned multiple courses. Apart from the things mentioned before, the team also did some analysis of the potential ethical effects, the market situation, and potential marketing tools. Part of the final submission was a video, which I will link here. The prototype is unfortunately not online anymore.
