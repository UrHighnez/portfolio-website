Covid Alarm'Dal Segno'

My new superpower

After the pandemic hit, most of the lectures had to happen via Zoom. This was especially unfortunate for the courses, that were going to teach us about electrical engineering. Howsoever, our professor decided to send all the equipment required to each of the students.

This way, everyone learned to solder together different components at their desk at home, designed their own circuit board and gave it into production, which was definitely one of my highlights during lockdown. I built a glove, that would make a sound according to the distance an ultra-sound-sensor measured. I also tried to be a little artistic about it and mapped the beeping sound to the keys of a piano and their lengths to those of notes. The video from the final presentation will be linked here.
