Map generation

Shaders and hexagons

Two small experiments in procedural terrain. The first was a map generator built to get comfortable with Blender's shader editor — the results were stunning, though I happily lost hours in the tiniest details.

The second, HexagonalLandscape, is a Unity project that builds hexagonal terrain from Perlin-noise heightmaps. Hexagon size, noise scale, height range and generation radius are all configurable, and the terrain streams in around the player's position.

A TileManager keeps track of the generated hexagons for efficient memory use, and a sample scene shows it in action.
