EmissionImpossible

Aka eco2y

Another project I did was the development of an app to track your emissions caused by transportation. According to the requirements, me and my teammate created the app for Android, writing in Java.

The final app was capable of calculating the different forms of public transport, as well as the type of fuel and the amount of passengers taken when driving a car. We also implemented a small diary to keep track of your trips.
