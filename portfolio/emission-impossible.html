EmissionImpossible

Tracking transportation emissions

A two-person project: an Android app, written in Java, that tracks the emissions caused by how you travel.

It estimates emissions for different modes of public transport, and for cars it factors in the fuel type and the number of passengers. A small trip diary keeps track of your journeys.
