Fun with Furniture

Designing workspaces

What was originally planned as a course to design and build furniture for the study location turned out to be a course about building smaller scale models of that furniture for your home office, because of the pandemic.

Something I came up with to showcase the features I had imagined for my model was a Stop-Motion-type film. I will link it here.

Since the study program was newly founded, a task during one of the earlier courses was building furniture made from cardboard for the mostly empty space. In addition to that, I decided to try out software I used ages ago during an internship as an architect, and loaded up sketchup to create a modular furniture design.

Also, I and my team created this smaller scale model, to gather some ideas. We also built some functional tables, triangular sitting pods and a ping-pong table.
