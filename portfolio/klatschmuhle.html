Klatschmühle

A covid party

During the pandemic, Shiny Toys, a festival for independent musicians, asked our program for new ways to give their artists a stage. My group built a digital 2D space where bumping your avatar into someone else makes a clapping sound.

Close, regular collaboration with the festival hosts shaped the result. The prototype actually got used: at the festival, a fake moving hand wired to an LED showed the digital 'claps' to the musicians on stage. Sadly it is no longer online.
